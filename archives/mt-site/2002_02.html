I don't get viruses damnit!

At least that's what I thought until I walked into work this morning.

Actually I didn't get a virus but when I walked into work this morning, my daily scan from Norton Antivirus assured me that I did.

It seems that the zlib dll that cygwin installs is now the same name as a virus backdoor.

Norton antivirus was happy to quarantine cygz.dll on my behalf, thinking that it was a virus. After assuring myself with a few google searches, I unquarantined the file and went on my merry way.

I'm actually going to fire off an email to both symantec and the cygwin folks about the whole thing.
