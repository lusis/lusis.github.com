New deftones

deftones 4.1.3

Actually I think it's been out for a while. I've been so busy that I didn't even know. 'Lucky You' is on the matrix reloaded soundtrack and that's what prompted me to hit deftones.com and see what was up.

I plan on buying the album here soon even if the some of the reviews are brutal. I've been warned not to expect "Adrenaline" or "Around The Fur" style material.

On another note, hit the videos section of deftones.com and watch the video for 'Minerva' and then watch 'Digital Bath'. I actually noticed this but it had been a while since I had seen Deftones live (hence watching the video for 'Digital Bath'), but has Chino gained some weight? I guess a few years off the road will do that. Not that it matters to me. Everyone knows I'm a big guy ;)

I just figured he got tired of looking like Ben Layer with the ski goggles in the D.B. video.
