Ann Coulter is a Cylon

So yesterday was my birthday.

Michelle got me an awesome t-shirt from Neighborhoodies.com.

I'll have pictures shortly but here's the gist:

We were watching Battlestar Galactica one night and getting annoyed over how Number 6 says the word "Gaius" and her random ramblings about religion. One of us (me allthough Michelle thinks she came up with it ;>) made the following comment:

"Ann Coulter is a Cylon"

It was a priceless moment that only a handful of people will probably get. We mentioned it to Joe last night when he called to wish me a happy birthday and he simply said "Wow. That is so true on so many levels".

So without further wait, I introduce my very own meme:

"Ann Coulter is a Cylon"

And if you're curious, I turned 31 yesterday.
