My userContent.css for Mozilla based browsers

So I added a few entries to the file recently and wanted to update them.

First off is a nice little hack to block flash ads. It applies to all flash content though. Any site that has flash on it, will get a button in place of the flash that says "Click here to play flash". It comes in handy on Yahoo! and a few other sites that abuse flash as an advertising medium.

The other hacks are more entries to block specific adserver strings.

If anyone has directions on how to get this working with IE or opera, I'd be interested to see it. Both browsers allow you to use a custom stylesheet but I think some of the lines in the file are gecko specific.
