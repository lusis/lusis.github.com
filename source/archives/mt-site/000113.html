Joe Job on my server

Well it looks like I've been Joe Job'd.

Looks like someone snagged an email address from google groups and they're using it to send out a few viruses. It's also possible that the sender is someone who was already infected. Alot of newer viruses are using I.E. cache files to grab email addresses. I searched the groups myself and didn't find any reference to this email address posted by pine. This also leads to another caution. Pine likes to insert some random information in the headers for internal use.

There are patches for pine to fix this but for now I'm telling my mailserver to block ANYTHING that is addressed to PINE*@* with a message about the Joe Job.
