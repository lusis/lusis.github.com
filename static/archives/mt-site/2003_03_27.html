Gilmore gets it WRONG

Well during my daily reading of cryptome, I found that the main link was in reagards to the U.S. media not showing the video and pictures of the POW's who were killed in Iraq.

I didn't realize that it actually had screengrabs. I'm a bit freaked out. Gilmore gets one thing wrong though. And being such a big point that he got wrong, I've lost alot of respect for him.

He signs the page with "Yours against censorship". He seems to think that the footage not being shown on US airwaves is one of censorship. It was not. I suggest he go and read up on what censorship is. Censorship is created by governments, not private businesses and organizations.

Is it censorship for me, as a private business owner, to not allow you to wear a shirt with some random message on it? No.

Is it censorship for the police to forbid you from saying or publishing the selfsame message? Yes.

The news media were asked by the administration NOT to air the footage. The networks were under no state restriction to hold it back.

The honored that request out of respect for families that might not have been notified that thier son or daughter was KIA. How would you like to see your brother or sister lying dead on the ground with a hole in the forehead while the jackass with the gun is standing there gloating? You wouldn't. Now imagine that being broadcast on national television. See the point?

Mr. Gilmore, you got it wrong sir. I feel for you not being able to see the difference. If your opposition to this conflict removes your respect for your fellow man, then you've lost your way.

There's an attempt to argue states rights (something I'm in favor of) and community-dictated standards.

The other side is that if this appeal is lost, it opens up the door to all sorts of government nosiness in the bedrooms of millions of americans. It also makes mention of a case I was unfamiliar with that involved an inter-racial relationship.

It started to get REALLY good right before the transcript was cut short.

It got the history wrong. It didn't understand the relationship of the sodomy to families, and, in addition, Bowers has proved to be harmful to thousands and thousands and thousands of people, if not because they're going to be prosecuted, because they fear it, they might be, which makes it a possible instrument of repression in the hands of the prosecutors.
