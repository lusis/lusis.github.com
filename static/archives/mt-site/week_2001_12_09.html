Gotta love RedHat!

Sometimes I wonder why I use redhat at all. Hell I started with Slackware.

Check out this little nugget of wisdom. It comes from a fresh install of Redhat 7.1 server. I was removing some unwanted packages that were not really needed (i.e. the rest of the kitchen sink)

In the process of removing some of above packages I had this little conundrum:

[root@demobox /root]# rpm -e ypbind
error: removing these packages would break dependencies:
ypbind is needed by yp-tools-2.4-7
[root@demobox /root]# rpm -e yp-tools
error: removing these packages would break dependencies:
yp-tools is needed by ypbind-1.7-6

Thank god for rpm -e --nodeps, eh?
